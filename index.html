CUSTOMER REWARD PROGRAM RULES:

  • New business customers who sign up for two or more eligible business services through ACN on or before April 30, 2018.
  • Eligible business services: ACN DigitalTalk® Express, XOOM Energy, Anovia Payments, DIRECTV, DISH Network, Vivint Smart Home and Flash Wireless. Flash Wireless customers must choose a business rate plan to qualify. An account in a business name using a consumer rate plan does not qualify. XOOM Energy customers must be a business type according to the utility. DISH Network, DIRECTV and Vivint Smart Home customers must be on business rate plans.
  • For multi-location businesses with common ownership, services from all Business Customer locations are eligible. Rewards will be paid to the identified main business address.
  • Customer must complete the online redemption form within 30 days of placing the orders. Once the services have been active and remain in good standing* for 120 days, the customer is eligible for the gift card. It may take up to 6 weeks after the 120-day period before receipt of the Reward™ Prepaid MasterCard®.
  • One Reward™ Prepaid MasterCard® per business.
  • Customers must use the Reward™ Prepaid MasterCard® within specified expiration period - 90 days.
